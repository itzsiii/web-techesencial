Tech Dopamine Fast: 24h Guide

Written by Iza Ogbinar • 3 min read

Do you check your phone right after waking up? If the answer is yes, your baseline dopamine levels are unregulated. Technology is not the enemy, but passive use of it is.

The Problem of "Infinite Novelty"

Apps like TikTok or Instagram exploit a flaw in our psychology: variable intermittent reinforcement. Every time you scroll, your brain expects a reward. When it gets it, it releases dopamine. When it doesn't, it creates anxiety so you keep looking.

The Solution: The "Boring" Setup

To reclaim your focus, you don't need to throw your phone in the river. You need to make it boring. Here is my 3-step protocol:

  • Grayscale Screen: Set your iPhone/Android to black and white. Colorful icons are candy for your brain. Without color, Instagram loses 90% of its appeal.
  • VIP Notifications: Only calls and calendar. Everything else (Whatsapp, Email) is checked in batches twice a day.
  • Physical Block: This is where the Sony WH-1000XM5 come in. Putting them on creates a physical barrier between you and the outside world.

"Productivity isn't doing more things, it's eliminating the distractions that stop you from doing what matters."

Conclusion

Try this for 24 hours. You will feel boredom, and that is good. Boredom is where creative ideas are born.
