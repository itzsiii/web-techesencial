White Noise vs. Silence: What's Better?

Jan 05, 2026 â€¢ 3 min read

There are two types of people: those who need tomb-like silence and those who need 'coffee shop noise' to concentrate.

Absolute silence can be bad

For brains with high activity (or ADHD), absolute silence can be deafening. When there are no external stimuli, the brain starts looking for them internally, generating distracting thoughts ("I have to do laundry", "I'm hungry").

The Brown Noise solution

"Brown Noise" (deeper than white noise) calms that restless part of the brain. It is like a sonic blanket that isolates you.

Recommended tools

If you are on team silence, Loop Quiet earplugs or Sony WH-1000XM5 headphones are mandatory to block out the world. If you are on team noise, try websites like Noisli or "Lofi Beats" playlists while you work.
