It's not your fault. There are engineers in Silicon Valley getting paid millions to design apps that hijack your attention.

Physical Friction

Willpower runs out throughout the day. The best strategy is not to resist, it is to create friction. Want to use your phone less? Don't trust yourself, use a Phone Stand and leave it there, far from your hand, or keep it in a drawer while you work.

The Grayscale Mode

A hacker trick: go to Settings > Accessibility and turn your phone to grayscale. Instagram without colors is incredibly boring. Red notifications no longer grab attention. Your brain will stop getting those visual dopamine hits and let you get back to focusing on what matters.
